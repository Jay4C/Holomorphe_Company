Dimensions, safety, quality and operation of the water electrolyser

The dimensions of the water electrolyser are defined by Holomorphe exclusively.


The water electrolyser will have a power supply of the order of some tens of watts.


Conditions of sale of the gas mixture of pure dihydrogen

The operating site must follow ATEX regulations for explosive atmospheres.


The operating site must follow all applicable regulations and standards applicable to its activity for the use of the gas mixture of pure dihydrogen.


The operating site must be insured by one or more insurer(s) in the event of damage for the use of the gas mixture of pure dihydrogen.


The operation site must respect a minimum annually consumption quota for the gas mixture of pure dihydrogen supplied by the Holomorphe at 500,000 cubic meters for its customers and 5,000,000 cubic meters in partnership.


The meter reading must be taken every month manually in presence of a staff of Holomorphe and a staff of the operating site run by a customer or a partner of Holomorphe . The records will be stored into a immutable database or a immutable ledger kept in safe by Holomorphe and a concerned customer or a concerned partner of Holomorphe .


The operating site must supply the water electrolyser with tap water at the expense of a concerned customer or a concerned partner of Holomorphe.


The operating site will have to supply the water electrolyser with electricity payable by a concerned customer or a concerned partner of Holomorphe.


Holomorphe will take out comprehensive professional civil liability insurance to insure itself against any damage caused by the use of its own water electrolyser in function in the operating site of the customer or partner of Holomorphe after examining the operation site by the insurance experts.


The price of pure dihydrogen will be sold in cubic meter at the standard conditions of temperature and pressure by Holomorphe with its partners.
The pure dihydrogen will be sold in function of the contract between Holomorphe, its partners and the buyers of this gas mixture.
